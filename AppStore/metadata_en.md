## Description (4000 chars max)

ScoreFrame lets you overlay scoreboards onto sports match videos and export them as polished MP4 files.

Whether it's your child's youth soccer game, a recreational league match, a handball tournament, or a rugby fixture, ScoreFrame transforms ordinary match footage into professional-looking broadcasts — complete with live-updating scoreboards.

KEY FEATURES
- Overlay scoreboards on recorded videos
- Record scores live with a built-in stopwatch (no video required)
- Record goals at exact timestamps in the video
- Set match timers (supports halves, extra time, and custom periods)
- Choose timer position (left or right side)
- Restart clock across periods (carry over timer value)
- Record and display penalty shootouts (PK)
- Penalty timer with countdown display (for sin-bins / suspensions)
- Timeout feature (auto-pauses match timer, shows timeout count)
- Customize scoreboard theme, colors, size, and position
- Merge multiple videos (e.g., first half + second half)
- Display match info (tournament name, date, etc.)
- Set team colors
- Export as MP4
- Save to photo library or share

HOW TO USE
1. Select your match video (or start without one)
2. Customize the scoreboard style
3. Play the video and record goal timings
4. Export and share

PERFECT FOR
- Parents recording their children's matches
- Coaches and managers archiving team footage
- Recreational players creating match highlights
- School teams sharing game videos

SUPPORTED SPORTS
Soccer, futsal, handball, rugby, hockey, basketball, and more
